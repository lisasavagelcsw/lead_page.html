Frequently Asked Questions

What age is this workbook for? ▼
This workbook is designed for children ages 4-10, but the activities can be adapted for older children as well. Parents and educators can modify the complexity based on the child's developmental level and needs.
How long does it take to complete? ▼
Each activity can be completed at the child's own pace, typically taking 5-10 minutes per page. There's no pressure to complete the entire workbook at once—children can work through it over several weeks or months.
Is this a substitute for therapy? ▼
No, this workbook is a complementary tool designed to support children's emotional development. For children with severe anxiety or mental health concerns, we recommend professional therapeutic support alongside using this resource.
Can I use this in my classroom? ▼
Absolutely! Teachers and educators use this workbook with individual students or entire classrooms. It's perfect for social-emotional learning (SEL) programs and can be incorporated into regular classroom activities.
What if my child doesn't like coloring? ▼
While the workbook is designed as a coloring book, the therapeutic value comes from the activities and prompts. Children can simply read through the pages, write responses, or engage with the activities in their own creative way.
